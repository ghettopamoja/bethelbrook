Welcome to BethelBrooke School

BethelBrook School is a Christian based school located in the serene, cool and quiet environs of the Ngong Forest next to the source of Ngong River at Gitwe, Kerarapon area

Our Mission

to provide holistic learning, nurture talents and develop skills geared to produce well equipped learners ready to face their dynamic future

Our Vision

To be a Dynamic school of choice providing high quality education, nurturing talents and developing skills with strong Christian morals and values

Our Featured Curriculum

The school offers Competence Based Curriculum (C.B.C), comprising of Day-care Unit, Pre-primary, Lower Primary, Upper Primary and Junior Schools

  • Languages: English, Kiswahili, and Indigenous Languages
  • Mathematics: Developing numeracy and problem-solving skills
  • Science and Technology: Fostering curiosity and scientific inquiry
  • Social Studies: Understanding the community, nation, and the world
  • Religious Education: Christian, Islamic, and Hindu Religious Education
  • Creative Arts: Art, craft, and music to enhance creativity and expression
  • Physical and Health Education: Promoting physical fitness and healthy living
  • Life Skills Education: Equipping learners with essential life skills
  • Technology Education: Introduction to ICT and its applications
  • Indigenous Knowledge: Integrating indigenous knowledge and skills

Our approach is learner-centered, ensuring that each child progresses according to their own abilities and interests. We believe in fostering a supportive and engaging learning environment where every student can thrive
